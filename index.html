About

A product studio for people who want to move faster.

Tija Labs is a productivity-focused product studio building apps that help people get more out of their time, whether they are studying for an exam, hunting for a job, growing early in their career, or picking up a new skill. We are just getting started, building our first lineup of products from the ground up.
